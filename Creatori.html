Cindy Sherman

Cynthia Morris Sherman (born January 19, 1954) is an American artist whose work consists primarily of photographic self-portraits, depicting herself in many different contexts and as various imagined characters. Her breakthrough work is often considered to be the collected "Untitled Film Stills," a series of 70 black-and-white photographs of herself evoking typical women roles in performance media (especially arthouse films and popular B-movies). In the 1980s, Sherman used color film and large prints, and focused more on costume, lighting and facial expression. In 1995, Sherman was the recipient of a MacArthur Fellowship. In 2013 she received an honorary doctorate degree from the Royal College of Art, London.

Takashi Murakami

Takashi Murakami (村上 隆, Murakami Takashi, born February 1, 1962) is a Japanese contemporary artist. He works in fine arts media (such as painting and sculpture) as well as commercial media (such as fashion, merchandise, and animation) and is known for blurring the line between high and low arts. He coined the term "superflat," which describes both the aesthetic characteristics of the Japanese artistic tradition and the nature of postwar Japanese culture and society, and is also used for Murakami's artistic style and other Japanese artists he has influenced.[1] Murakami is the founder and President of Kaikai Kiki Co., Ltd., through which he manages several younger artists. He was the founder and organizer of the biannual art fair Geisai.

Mircea Cantor

Artist român născut în 1977, este un nume pe care l-am descoperit în 2005, anul în care propunea în cadrul expoziţiei Departure, lucrarea Diamond Corn. Lucrarea era formată din 20 de ştiuleţi de porumb turnaţi în cristal, însoţiţi de două cartoane ondulate, pliate pentru a forma un suport simplu care pune în evidenţă luminozitatea elementelor expuse deasupra. Printr-un astfel de joc simplitate (cartonul ondulat) – eleganţă (cristalul ca material din care sunt confecţionaţi stiuleţii de porumb), artistul încearcă să evoce valoarea economică şi nutritivă a porumbului. Un mesaj atât de clar transmis printr-o lucrare de artă insolită.
